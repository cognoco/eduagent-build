With paused topics
Why this works: The three sections each answer one unambiguous question —
"Continue now" → the thing I'm in the middle of (max 1) · "Paused" → threads I left hanging · "Up next" → the next new topic
What about recommender logic? The curriculum's suggestedNextId stays purely forward-looking — it's always pointing at a new topic the learner hasn't started. If we later want to gently nudge learners to finish paused topics, that's a separate feature (e.g., a subtle "You have 2 paused topics — resume one?" banner near the top) — not a mutation of "Up next".
