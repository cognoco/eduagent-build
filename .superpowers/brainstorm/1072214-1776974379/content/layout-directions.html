Biggest departure: a hero card answers "what now?" without scanning. Recent sessions sit just below as a motivation strip. Full list is flat (no chapter boxes) with explicit state on every row.

What's common to all three:
  • The flame icon is gone — replaced by explicit labels (DOING, NEXT) and state dots (✓ ● → ○)
  • The old "Study next" cards are removed — they duplicated the list
  • Past sessions move higher so "proof of progress" doesn't require scrolling
  • Header is tighter — recovers vertical space
