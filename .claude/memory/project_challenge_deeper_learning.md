---
name: Challenge means deeper active learning
description: Use when designing, implementing, or testing Challenge, Review, Quiz, or mastery-assessment surfaces.
type: project
---

Product ruling (Zuzka, 2026-07-17): **Challenge extends the current learning-session topic with harder or additional source-grounded material.** It asks the learner to interpret, apply, compare, explain, or defend an answer.

Challenge is distinct from Review, Quiz, and mastery assessment. It must not switch to unrelated people, launch a generic recall game, or simply repeat facts taught moments earlier. Literature challenges may use short licensed, permitted, or learner-provided excerpts to deepen a theme or technique.

**Canonical records:** OPQ-118 (Closed) and WI-2112, “Redefine Challenge as deeper active learning instead of a recall quiz.” The WI acceptance criteria carry the implementation and test details.
