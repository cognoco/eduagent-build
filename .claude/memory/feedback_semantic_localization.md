---
name: Semantic localization, not literal translation
description: Use when translating or reviewing user-visible MentoMate copy in any supported UI language.
type: feedback
---

Translations must communicate the intended meaning and mentor tone naturally in
the target language. A grammatically valid word-for-word translation is not an
acceptable completion criterion.

**Why:** During the 2026-07-15 German device pass, copy such as `Ask anything` →
`Frag alles` was technically recognizable but unnatural and too literal in its
screen context.

**How to apply:** Review complete screens and flows, not isolated keys. Compare
the English product intent with native target-language phrasing; check tone,
idiom, pronouns, product jargon, and the surrounding CTA/placeholder relationship.
Treat machine-generated translations as drafts requiring semantic review.
