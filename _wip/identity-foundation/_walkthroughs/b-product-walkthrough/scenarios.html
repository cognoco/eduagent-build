Identity Foundation — Scenario Sketches

Two situations from the walkthrough, drawn before → after.
Illustrative only — not the data model. These show what happens to people in two situations, to support the conversation. No storage detail implied.

① The last consenting parent leaves (Segment 4)

The child and their learning are never lost. The open question is the product behaviour: hold them safely and let another verified adult attach.

② Two people become one family (Segment 5)

A teen who signed up alone joins a parent who already has a family. To the teen it's “join my family”; underneath, they move into the parent's family and the solo account is retired. The child asks; the verified adult accepts — never the reverse.

Nuance to surface: if the teen still needs a parent's yes, the parent becomes the consent-giver; if the teen is old enough to decide alone, the parent is just the payer / a helper — not automatically allowed to see the work.
