Identity Foundation — Model Diagrams

Visual companion to domain-model-options.md · DRAFT, leading hypothesis · 2026-06-01

1 · Entity graph §2–§9

The candidate domain model: one tenancy graph anchored on a credential-optional Person. Clerk supplies authentication only (the optional Credential); Neon owns everything else. Guardianship is a first-class, capability-bearing edge between two Persons.

Person ≠ Credential — Credential is 0..1 and optional; a proxied child is a Person with none. §3, §4
Membership — M:N Person↔Org, carries a role set; multi-org is native. ORG-08
Guardianship — global guardian → charge edge; consent authority is global, operational caps (proxy/manage/view) are org-scoped via Membership. §9
Consent — required = f(age, jurisdiction), satisfied is jurisdiction-scoped + append-only; never a stamped boolean. §8.3
Subscription — held by the Organization (Stripe customer = org). §2
Role → Permission — capability matrix as data, replacing isOwner checks. RC-07

2 · Cohort & transition state machine §4, §8

Cohorts are positions on two axes (Credential yes/no · consent-gated yes/no), separated by two age thresholds. Edges are the transitions T1–T6. Time-triggered moves (T2, T3) need a scheduler (Inngest), not a request handler — a dormant account still transitions on a birthday.

T1 proxied → credentialed — child gets a login; a Credential late-binds to the existing Person (no new user). action
T2 consent gate lifts — crosses the consent threshold by age. time
T3 graduation at 18 — authority transfers automatically; org/billing migration is offered (interim state). time · PPA-R11
T3b proxied → adult — forces a credential; if never claimed → Dormant adult. T1+T3
T4 jurisdiction change — gate re-engages (stricter) → suspend pending consent, or lifts (looser). No birthday. action
T6 de-credential — shown dotted; likely disallowed, do not build speculatively.
⚠ self-register entry — a minor signs up with no guardian yet → who consents? (drift-map §7A)
Worst-case default collapses the gated/ungated split at launch, making T4 a non-event until you relax per-jurisdiction. §8.2
