## What's LOCKED (ontology v1)

- **Entities:** **Person** (the human; RBAC-human/ABAC-subject), **Organization** (thin tenant; "Family" =
  a label), **Login** (the authentication binding to a Clerk User; 0..1 per Person; `Credential` rejected).
- **Tenancy fork (C7, ADR 0001):** own the Person/Org/Membership/Guardianship graph in **Neon**; **Clerk =
  authentication only** (never Organizations-as-tenancy). The age-consent spike's "Clerk Orgs for access"
  line is **superseded** — reconcile at fold-in.
- **Membership** carries a **role set `{admin, learner}`** (only these two are roles). First member = `admin`.
- **Roles vs capacities vs edges:** *roles* (admin, learner) = your standing in an org; *edges*
  (**Guardianship**, **Mentorship**) = one-to-one ties; *capacities* (`guardian`/`charge`,
  `mentor`/`mentee`) = the ends of edges. **`mentor` and `guardian` are NOT roles.**
- **Two-layer model:** **Guardianship** = consent authority (Layer 1, establishes lawful basis,
  withdrawable); **Mentorship** = granted visibility/help (Layer 2, edge-scoped, never org-wide). A mentor
  never holds consent authority; mentorship is authorized by the mentee if consent-capable, else the guardian.
- **Far-end term:** **`charge`** (lowercase) ≡ "consent-gated learner" (technical synonym). **No "ward".**
- **Two independent dimensions:** *Login-presence* (managed vs credentialed) ⊥ *consent-requirement*
  (`requiresGuardianConsent`, age × jurisdiction — never an `isMinor` boolean). A charge may be managed OR
  credentialed.
- **`minor` = under-18** (the contract/**Payer** rung; Payer ≥ 18, a Subscription designation, not a role) —
  distinct from the consent rung; never a code gate.
- **The AI is the "Mate"** (MentoMATE); `mentor` = the **human** capacity. (Copy sweep pending — CLEANUP-2.)
- **§4 invariants (21)** ratified as the definition of done.
