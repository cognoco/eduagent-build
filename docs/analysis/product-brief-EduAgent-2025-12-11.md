# Product Brief: EduAgent

**Date:** 2025-12-11
**Author:** Zuzka

---

## Executive Summary

EduAgent is a premium AI tutoring platform designed for motivated learners aged 11+ who need structured, verified learning outcomes—not passive content consumption. We address a critical gap: while video courses, gamified drills, and generic AI chatbots flood the market, none combine affordability with actual teaching that produces lasting results.

**The Opportunity:** The AI tutoring market is growing at 30.5% CAGR ($1.6-3.5B in 2024 → $6.5-8.0B by 2030), driven by proven effectiveness (25-30% academic performance gains) and massive cost arbitrage (€30/month vs €60-100/hour for human tutors). However, the market window is narrowing rapidly—OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic launched free tutoring modes in mid-2025, commoditizing core features.

**Our Approach:** Position as a craft-scale business serving a focused segment willing to pay €30/month for depth over breadth. Target: €50K ARR with 280 paying users. Unlike free alternatives that lack structure and verification, EduAgent provides persistent curricula, systematic assessment, and learning continuity—features that create switching costs and defensibility.

**Key Metrics:** 15%+ trial-to-paid conversion, 40%+ 30-day retention, with validation through beta users. The window for differentiated entry is 6-12 months before free platforms add structured learning features.

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

**No affordable option exists that actually teaches—with memory, structure, verification, and personalization.**

Learning today is broken because existing solutions optimize for engagement or scale, not outcomes:
- Video courses (Coursera, Udemy) enable passive consumption with no verification
- Gamified drills (Duolingo) teach pattern matching, not understanding
- Generic AI chat (ChatGPT, Gemini) lacks memory, structure, or curriculum persistence
- Human tutors cost €30-80/hour with scheduling friction and inconsistent quality
- Homework helpers (Gauth, Answer AI) give answers rather than teaching

The critical insight: learners don't need more information—they need guided practice, systematic verification, and continuity across sessions. Current solutions provide none of these at an accessible price point.

### Problem Impact

**For motivated learners:**
- Parents pay €60-100/hour for human tutors, unsustainable for regular use
- Career changers struggle to find structured guidance for skill acquisition
- Students fail courses despite watching hours of video lectures
- Certification seekers lack effective preparation that ensures retention

**Market validation:** 86% of higher education students now use AI for learning, but completion rates and retention remain poor. The gap between consumption and mastery is widening—students access more content than ever but learn less effectively.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

**Free AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude):**
- No structured, persistent curricula across sessions
- No systematic verification of understanding
- No progress tracking or learning journey persistence
- Memory exists but isn't organized into teachable sequences

**Established EdTech:**
- Khanmigo ($4/month): K-12 focused, doesn't serve adult learners or career changers
- Duolingo Max ($30/month): Narrow vertical (languages only), 545K users proves pricing viability
- Quizlet: Flashcard-based memorization without comprehension verification
- Coursera: Passive video courses with minimal engagement

**Human Tutoring:**
- €30-80/hour creates cost barrier for consistent use
- Quality varies dramatically by tutor
- Scheduling friction limits accessibility
- No scalability for learners with multiple knowledge gaps

**The Gap:** No solution combines research-backed pedagogy, personalized memory, structured curriculum, and systematic verification at an accessible price point for serious learners.

### Proposed Solution

**"A teacher who grows with you."**

EduAgent is an AI-powered personal tutoring platform that teaches through conversation—using research-backed methodologies to guide learners to understanding, remembering what they've learned across sessions, and verifying retention.

**Four Pillars:**

1. **Memory:** Remembers what you learned, your struggles, and how you learn best—eliminating redundant explanations and building on prior knowledge

2. **Structure:** Builds personalized curricula and sequences topics in optimal order—not random Q&A but a guided learning journey

3. **Verification:** Tests understanding through learner-generated demonstrations of mastery and systematic assessment—you can't just consume, you must prove comprehension

4. **Relationship:** Knows your goals, learning style, and progress history—a persistent teacher, not a disposable tool

**The Solution Approach:**

Rather than competing with free AI on breadth, EduAgent focuses on depth for motivated learners. We serve the segment willing to pay €30/month for structured learning that produces verified outcomes—parents paying for children's education, career changers investing in earning potential, and certification seekers who need results.

**Positioning:** Premium AI tutoring at 1/10th the cost of human tutors. We create defensibility through persistent learning infrastructure—curricula that follow learners across sessions, progress visibility for families, and regulatory compliance that respects young learners' privacy.

Unlike answer-giving bots, EduAgent requires learners to demonstrate understanding before progressing, creating verified learning outcomes rather than passive consumption.

### Key Differentiators

**What Big Tech Lacks (Our Moat):**

| Gap in Free AI | EduAgent Solution | Switching Cost |
|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| No persistent curriculum | Structured syllabus across sessions | HIGH (lose learning path) |
| No verification of learning | Systematic mastery demonstration requirements | HIGH (no proof of mastery) |
| No progress tracking | Mastery scores with knowledge decay monitoring | MEDIUM (lose history) |
| No parental oversight | Family accounts with learning visibility | MEDIUM (parent access) |
| No compliance for minors | GDPR-compliant data handling for children | HIGH (regulatory barrier) |

**Competitive Positioning:**

- **vs Free AI (ChatGPT, Gemini):** Structure + Memory + Verification creates persistence and accountability
- **vs Khanmigo ($4/month):** Serves adult learners and career changers, not just K-12
- **vs Duolingo Max ($30/month):** Any subject, not just languages; 545K users proves premium pricing viable
- **vs Human Tutors (€60-100/hour):** Same teaching quality, 1/10th the price, available 24/7

**Data Moat:** User-created learning history with personalized curricula and mastery scores creates high switching costs—starting over elsewhere means losing all progress and learning continuity.

### User Journey

**1. Discovery**
- User downloads app from App Store / Play Store or visits web app

**2. Onboarding (5 minutes)**
- Create account (Email, Google, Apple, or OpenAI)
- Profile setup: name, birthdate, country, avatar
- Intent fork: "Learn something new" OR "Get homework help"
- For learning path: Subject input → Interview (~3 min) → AI generates curriculum
- For homework: Subject selection → Problem input → Direct to chat

**3. First Lesson (10-15 minutes)**
- AI introduces first topic
- Conversational teaching (chat interface)
- User asks questions, AI adapts
- Quick check: "Let me see if you got that..."
- Summary generated and saved

**4. Daily Engagement**
- Notification: "Ready for your next lesson?"
- Continue from last topic
- AI references prior learning: "Remember when..."
- Track progress, earn Retention XP
- Review past lessons from summaries

**5. Retention Loop**
- "It's been a week—want a quick review?"
- Re-test from saved summaries
- Spaced repetition keeps knowledge fresh
- Honest Streak motivates real review (not just app opens)

## Business Model

### Pricing Strategy

> **⚠️ CANONICAL SOURCE:** Full pricing specification in `docs/Legacy/eduagent-pricing-specification.md`. This section summarizes key decisions.

**4-Tier Model:** Free → Plus → Family → Pro

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Users | Questions/Month |
|------|---------|--------|-------|-----------------|
| **Free** | €0 | - | 1 | 50* |
| **Plus** | €18.99 | €169 (26% off) | 1 | 500 |
| **Family** | €28.99 | €259 (26% off) | Up to 4 | 1,500 shared |
| **Pro** | €48.99 | €439 (25% off) | Up to 6 | 3,000 shared |

*\*Free tier includes first-week boost: 10 questions/day for days 1-7*

**Key Strategic Decisions:**
1. **Gate usage, not content:** All features free; only question limits differentiate tiers
2. **Shared pools:** Family/Pro share questions across users (creates upgrade pressure)
3. **Charm pricing:** All prices end in .99 (24% conversion lift)
4. **First-week boost:** Free users get 10/day for 7 days to reach "aha moment"
5. **Reverse trial:** Soft landing after trial (15/day for 14 days) vs hard cliff

**Top-Up Credits:**
- Plus: €10/500 questions
- Family/Pro: €5/500 questions (loyalty reward)
- Expiration: 12 months from purchase

**Trial Strategy:**
- 14 days full Plus access
- Credit card required (2.7x higher conversion)
- Reverse trial: Days 15-28 get 15 questions/day before dropping to Free

### Unit Economics

**Revenue Targets:**
- **Goal:** €50,000 ARR (~280 paid subscribers at €170 ARPU)
- **Break-even:** ~140 users

**Margin Analysis (Annual Pricing):**

## MVP Scope

### Core Features (v1.0)

**Authentication & Onboarding:**
- Email + Google + Apple + OpenAI Sign-In
- OpenAI auth option (exploring): enables user-funded LLM token usage, reducing EduAgent API costs—implementation details TBD
- Age verification (11+ minimum)
- GDPR parental consent flow (ages 11-15 in EU)
- COPPA parental consent flow (ages 11-12 in US)
- Multi-profile support (family accounts under one subscription)
- Intent Screen: "Learn something new" vs "Get homework help"

**AI-Powered Learning:**
- Dynamic curriculum generation for ANY subject
- Subject viability assessment (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW confidence)
- **Adaptive teaching methodology:**
  - Default: Socratic method (guide learner to discover)
  - Struggle detection: If learner cannot progress, AI may explain directly
  - Always verify: Learner must explain back in their own words
  - Language subjects: Four Strands methodology (explicit instruction + input + output + fluency)
  - **Architecture: Teaching methods configurable/tunable at backend—design for pluggable strategies**
- Conversational learning with streaming responses
- Prior knowledge injection across sessions

**Learning Outcome Safeguards:**
- Mandatory user production (learner-generated summaries)
- AI-guided self-correction before providing answers
- Conversation guardrails with invisible objective tracking
- Parking lot for deferred questions

**Homework Integrity Mode (Killer Feature for Parents):**
- AI guides through problems, NEVER gives final answers
- Socratic questioning: "What do you think the first step is?"
- Sessions marked as "guided problem-solving" in Learning Book
- Parent visibility: proof child did the thinking

**Progress & Retention:**
- Topic summaries auto-generated and stored
- Mastery levels per topic with decay tracking
- Re-testing from saved summaries (spaced repetition)
- Outcome-based gamification: Honest Streak, Retention XP, knowledge decay bars

**Code Execution (Programming Subjects):**
- Browser-based code execution (technology TBD—e.g., WebAssembly-based solutions)
- Inline code editor with real-time execution
- Test cases (visible + hidden) for exercises

**Platforms:**
- iOS, Android, Web
- UI languages: English, German (MVP); architecture prepared for additional languages
- Learning/tutoring: Any language the user desires
- Backend: English only
