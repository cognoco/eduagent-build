Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Pre-launch draft: this notice is not approved for publication. The applicable provider contracts, transfer safeguards, DPO details, retention controls, and native-speaker legal reviews must be completed before learner data is processed in production.

1. Who We Are

MentoMate is an AI-powered tutoring platform operated by ZWIZZLY AS, Norwegian organisation number 811 696 072, registered at Fiskekroken 3B, 0139 Oslo, Norway ("we", "us", "our"). MentoMate is for learners aged 13 and older. Because some of our users are minors, we apply additional protections to children's data as described below.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: email address, display name, date of birth, declared country of habitual residence, and language and pronoun preferences you provide during registration. Before an EEA country is enabled, habitual residence is used to select the applicable national consent threshold. Missing or conflicting residence information fails closed and prevents learning-data processing until it is resolved.

Learning data: the subjects you study, your interview and onboarding responses, curriculum and topic progress, the messages exchanged during learning sessions, assessment and mastery results, review scores, and XP. Some of this data is used to build a persistent "learning memory" so the tutor can remember what you have already studied (see Section 8).

Device data: push notification tokens, device type, and app version for delivering notifications and maintaining service quality.

Camera & microphone: The app requests camera access for homework images and microphone access for voice-based learning. Microphone input is converted to text on your device; MentoMate does not receive or retain the audio recording. We use these inputs only when you explicitly activate them.

We do not collect precise device location or GPS data, contacts, your photo library other than homework images you explicitly select, or browsing history. The declared country of habitual residence described above is not continuous device-location tracking.

3. How We Use Your Data

To provide personalised AI tutoring that explains concepts and checks your understanding, and to schedule review using spaced repetition. To generate curricula, coaching insights, and progress reports. To do this we build an adaptive profile of your learning — what you have mastered, where you struggle, and how you learn best — and use it to tailor tuition to you. This profiling is used only to personalise your teaching; it does not produce legal or similarly significant effects about you. To send review reminders and notifications you have opted into. To maintain and improve our service through error and performance monitoring. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it.

4. Parental Consent (GDPR)

MentoMate is unavailable to users under 13 at launch. The intended launch perimeter covers all 30 EEA countries, but each country remains disabled until its legal and technical gates are complete. Within enabled EEA countries, the age at which a minor may consent to an online service's consent-based processing varies from 13 to 16 by country of habitual residence. Where a user is below that national threshold, verified authorization from the holder of parental responsibility is required before learning data is collected. In France, users aged 13–14 and the holder of parental responsibility must both consent, and both grants must be recorded. The United Kingdom, United States, and all other non-EEA markets are disabled unless separately cleared.

Parents can withdraw consent at any time. Consent can be restored during a 7-day grace period. At the end of that period, profiles aged 13 or younger are deleted; older child profiles may instead be archived for 30 days, according to the account owner's retention preference, and are then deleted.

5. AI Processing

Learning-session content is processed by third-party AI model providers to generate educational responses. We send only the content and context needed to generate a response. For minors, we exclude names and account identifiers from content sent to AI providers. For unambiguously adult account owners, a display name may be included for personalisation. Our launch requirement is that providers do not use customer content to train their general-purpose models. The contracts and account settings supporting that requirement must be verified for every active provider before learner data is processed in production.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the service providers (processors) needed to run MentoMate:

  • Authentication provider (Clerk) for sign-in;
  • AI model and embedding providers for learning responses and learning memory;
  • Database and application hosting providers (Neon and Cloudflare);
  • Email provider (Resend) for notifications;
  • Error and performance monitoring (Sentry) for app stability;
  • Push-notification providers (Expo, Apple, and Google);
  • Payment and subscription providers (Apple, Google, and RevenueCat).

7. International Data Transfers

Some of our processors are located outside the EEA, including in the United States. Before any international transfer of learner data occurs, we will establish and document appropriate safeguards under the GDPR — such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy mechanism such as the EU–US Data Privacy Framework where the provider is certified. Production learner data will not be routed internationally until the applicable safeguards have been verified.

8. Data Retention

Age and residence: date of birth and the current declared country of habitual residence are kept while the account is active so the correct age and consent rules can be enforced. A limited history of age/residence assertions may be retained for accountability; its final retention period must be approved before this notice is published.

Chat transcripts: the full text of your learning-session messages is retained for 30 days after the learning summary is generated and then automatically deleted. If summary processing is delayed, operational safeguards flag the delay for resolution.

Learning memory: a summary of what you have studied — your subjects, progress, mastery results, and the notes and recaps generated from your sessions — is kept for as long as your account is active, so the tutor can remember your learning over time. Some notes and recaps may include short quotations from your own session messages. MentoMate does not use this learning memory for advertising or marketing profiles. Our launch requirement is that third-party AI providers do not use customer content to train their general-purpose models; the provider controls supporting that requirement must be verified before production learner data is processed.

Account deletion: when you delete your account, deletion enters a 7-day grace period during which you can cancel. After the grace period, your account and associated personal and learning data are permanently removed, except for limited records retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or accounting reasons.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data (via the Export feature in Settings); correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your account and associated personal and learning data, subject to the limited retention exceptions described in Section 8; withdraw consent for data processing; object to processing; and request data portability.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at support@mentomate.com.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication, and profile-scoped data isolation ensuring each user can only access their own data.

11. Contact

ZWIZZLY AS is the controller of your personal data. For privacy questions or to exercise your data-protection rights, email support@mentomate.com or write to ZWIZZLY AS, Fiskekroken 3B, 0139 Oslo, Norway.
