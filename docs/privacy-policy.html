Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

MentoMate is an AI-powered tutoring platform operated by Cognoco s.r.o. ("we", "us", "our"). MentoMate is for learners of all ages. Because some of our users are minors, we apply additional protections to children's data as described below. You must be at least 13 years old to use MentoMate.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: email address, display name, year of birth, and language and pronoun preferences you provide during registration.

Learning data: the subjects you study, your interview and onboarding responses, curriculum and topic progress, the messages exchanged during learning sessions, assessment and mastery results, review scores, and XP. Some of this data is used to build a persistent "learning memory" so the tutor can remember what you have already studied (see Section 7).

Device data: push notification tokens, device type, and app version for delivering notifications and maintaining service quality.

Camera & microphone: The app requests camera access to photograph homework and microphone access for voice-based learning. Microphone input is transcribed to text for the session and is not retained as an audio recording. We use these inputs only when you explicitly activate them.

We do NOT collect location data, contacts, your photo library (other than homework images you explicitly capture), or browsing history.

3. How We Use Your Data

To provide personalised AI tutoring that explains concepts and checks your understanding, and to schedule review using spaced repetition. To generate curricula, coaching insights, and progress reports. To do this we build an adaptive profile of your learning — what you have mastered, where you struggle, and how you learn best — and use it to tailor tuition to you. This profiling is used only to personalise your teaching; it does not produce legal or similarly significant effects about you. To send review reminders and notifications you have opted into. To maintain and improve our service through error and performance monitoring. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it.

4. Parental Consent (GDPR & COPPA)

Where a user is below the applicable age of digital consent — which is between 13 and 16 in the EU/EEA depending on the country, and 13 in the United Kingdom and under COPPA in the United States — we require verifiable parental consent before processing the child's personal data. A parent or guardian must approve the child's account before any learning data is collected.

Parents can withdraw consent at any time from the parent dashboard. Upon withdrawal, the child's data enters a 7-day grace period and is then permanently deleted.

5. AI Processing

Learning sessions are processed by third-party AI model providers to generate educational responses. Session content may be sent to these providers for processing under contractual terms (data processing agreements) that prohibit them from using your content to train their models. We share only the learning content needed to generate a response, not your account identity.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the service providers (processors) needed to run MentoMate:

  • Authentication provider (Clerk) for sign-in;
  • AI model providers for generating learning responses;
  • Email provider (Resend) for notifications;
  • Error and performance monitoring (Sentry) for app stability;
  • App-store payment and subscription providers (Apple, Google, and RevenueCat) for managing subscriptions.

7. International Data Transfers

Some of our processors are located outside the EU/EEA and the UK, including in the United States. Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards under the GDPR and UK GDPR — such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, or an adequacy mechanism such as the EU–US Data Privacy Framework where the provider is certified.

8. Data Retention

Chat transcripts: the full text of your learning-session messages is retained for 30 days and then automatically deleted.

Learning memory: a summary of what you have studied — your subjects, progress, mastery results, and the notes and recaps generated from your sessions — is kept for as long as your account is active, so the tutor can remember your learning over time. Some notes and recaps may include short quotations from your own session messages. This learning memory is used only to provide tutoring continuity to you; it is not used for advertising, for marketing profiles, or to train third-party AI models.

Account deletion: when you delete your account, deletion enters a 7-day grace period during which you can cancel. After the grace period, all of your data — including your learning memory and your login credentials — is permanently removed.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data (via the Export feature in Settings); correct inaccurate data; delete your account and all associated data; withdraw consent for data processing; object to processing; and request data portability.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at privacy@mentomate.com.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication, and profile-scoped data isolation ensuring each user can only access their own data.

11. Contact & Data Protection Officer

Cognoco s.r.o. is the controller of your personal data. For privacy questions, or to contact our Data Protection Officer, email privacy@mentomate.com.
